Population


The population of London Ontario is 383822 people.
The average age is 38 years.

The City

The outside of London is mostly farms.
The middle area is mostly houses and shops.
Downtown london however is where all the skyscrapers are.
There are many things in downtown london, like a outdoor skating rink and the city hall. The current mayor is Matt Brown.

Crime

There is not a lot of crime in this city.
The area which has a lot of crime is East of Adele.
The police force are called london police
